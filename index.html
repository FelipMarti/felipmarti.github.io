About me

My name is Felip, and I have two surnames Martí Carrillo (It's crazy!... isn't it?), no middle name. So, to make it easier just Felip Martí (Yes, just the first surname is fine).

I am currently a PhD student at Swinburne University of Technology under the principal supervision of Dr Chris McCarthy, and with the support of Data61 at CSIRO, Melbourne. I received a Bachelor's degree in Computer Systems Engineering and a Master's degree in Robotics from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). My final master's thesis was completed on exchange at Sweden's Lund University, LTH, Department of Computer Sciences under the supervision of Dr Elin Anna Topp.

Research Areas: Human-Robot Interaction, Machine Learning, Computer Vision.
