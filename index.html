About me

I am a Research Engineer in Internet of Things (IoT) working at the Swinburne's Digital Innovation Lab. Under the guidance of A/Prof. Prem Prakash Jayaraman, I am leading the research and development of industry projects related to AgriTech.

My PhD explored the integration of a general purpose social robot for ongoing clinical deployment. It was pursed under the principal supervision of Dr Chris McCarthy, and with the support of Data61 at CSIRO, Melbourne. In a close partnership with the Royal Children's Hospital in Melbourne, we explored how the NAO robot can socially assist paediatric rehabilitation in an existing clinical practice.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Systems Engineering and a Master's degree in Robotics from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). My final master's thesis was completed on exchange at Sweden's Lund University, LTH, Department of Computer Sciences under the supervision of Dr Elin Anna Topp.

Research Areas: Human-Robot Interaction, Internet of Things, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, AgriTech.
