About me

I am a PhD student (currently under examination) at Swinburne University of Technology under the principal supervision of Dr Chris McCarthy, and with the support of Data61 at CSIRO, Melbourne.

My PhD explores the integration of a general purpose social robot for ongoing clinical deployment. In a close partnership with the Royal Children's Hospital in Melbourne we are developing software for the NAO robot to socially assist paediatric rehabilitation in an existing clinical practice.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Systems Engineering and a Master's degree in Robotics from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). My final master's thesis was completed on exchange at Sweden's Lund University, LTH, Department of Computer Sciences under the supervision of Dr Elin Anna Topp.

Research Areas: Human-Robot Interaction, Internet of Things, Machine Learning, Computer Vision.
