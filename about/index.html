About me

My name is Felip, and I have two surnames Martí Carrillo (It's crazy!... isn't it?), no middle name. So, to make it easier just Felip Martí (Yes, just the first surname is fine).

I am a PhD Student at Swinburne University of Technology and and Data61, CSIRO, Melbourne, Australia. I received a Bachelor's degree in Computer Systems Engineering and a Master's degree in Robotics from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). I completed my final Master's thesis on exchange at Sweden's Lund University, LTH, Department of Computer Sciences.

Research Areas: Human-Robot Interaction, Machine Learning, Computer Vision.
